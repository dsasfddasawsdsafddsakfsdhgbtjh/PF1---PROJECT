    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I place an order?

    Browse our shop, select your favorite items, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can create an account or continue as a guest. Follow the payment prompts to complete your purchase.

    What is the return policy?

    We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Visit our returns page for more details.

    How long does shipping take?

    Standard shipping typically takes 5-10 business days. For faster delivery, we offer express shipping options at checkout. Check our shipping page for more information.

    Can I use a discount code?

    Yes! We regularly offer discount codes through our email newsletter and social media. Enter your code at checkout to apply savings to your order.

    What if I forgot my password?

    You can reset your password on our login page. Click "Forgot your password?" and follow the instructions to create a new password.

    How do I track my order?

    Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also check your order status in your account. For more help, visit our orders page.

    Do you offer gift cards?

    Yes! We offer digital and physical gift cards. Learn how to purchase and use them on our gift cards page.
